Cell Shades / Honeycomb Shades

Creates a welcoming environment by offering many features, making them the ideal choice to adorn windows of many shapes and sizes. Cellular shades help establish a climate of energy efficiency in many indoor settings by blocking the sun's UV rays, keeping the room cool in the summer while simultaneously trapping temperature controlled air which helps maintain warm rooms warm in the winter. Enticing colors and fabrics combine with a host of specialty systems to compliment any home décor. Neutral white backing ensures uniform appearance to the outside of the home. Widths as large as 120” and up to 336” for vertical shade application promise this product can cover even the largest expanses with no visible seams or overlaps.

Design Options :- With many different choices in operation of the shade, this product will fit any preference within the household.

Privacy and Light Control :- Our shades comes in two fabric types: translucent and blackout. Translucent fabrics allow privacy while still transmitting some of the natural light. Blackout fabrics blocks 99% of the light providing considerable privacy and would be ideal for either a media room or a child's room.

Child Safety :- To maximize child safety, we offer two means of operating shades without cords. A deluxe cordless system allows the homeowner to manually adjust the shade up and down by simply raising or lowering on the shade. The other cord free option is motorization.

Arches :- Movable and stationary arches are the perfect compliment for rooms containing our shades shades.

Motorization :- For the hard to reach locations or to maximize child safety, battery powered motorized options are available on cellular shades. A motor will eliminate the dangling cord to maintain maximum safety for kids and pets while providing the cleanest and smoothest look to the window shade. Many upgrades are available with the motorized lift system including a battery pack, satellite eye and AC adaptor.
