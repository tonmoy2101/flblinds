ABOUT FLORIDA BLINDS

Florida Blinds provides made-to-measure blinds, shades and shutters to customers in their homes throughout Central Florida. We also specialise in custom made to measure commercial window treatments. It's not just the years' experience that sets us apart from the rest. It's the creativity and beautiful product range that allows us to transform any window into a masterpiece.

Our People

Working at Florida Blinds means being part of a dynamic business that recognises the contributions of everyone, whatever their position. There are plenty of opportunities to progress and to develop a role. And as a major local employer with many positions available, Florida Blinds gives the chance to leap into something completely different if you have the determination to succeed. From hands on production & Install roles to opportunities within Sales, Marketing, HR and Accounting. there are so many reasons to make a career with Florida Blinds.

Our advisors
